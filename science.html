Science

Science at ABC School is not just taught; it is experienced. We house fully equipped, safety-certified laboratories to ensure our students learn by actively "doing" rather than just passive reading.

Biology

Microscopy, human anatomy, genetics, botany, and ecological field studies within our campus botanical garden.

Chemistry

Periodic trends, organic syntheses, titration experiments, and molecular modeling in our fume-hood equipped labs.

Physics

Mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, and optics. We utilize modern sensors and data logging equipment for precise measurements.
