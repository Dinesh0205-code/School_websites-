Books and Library

"A library is not a luxury but one of the necessities of life."
Our library stands as the intellectual hub of ABC School.

Physical Collection

Over 15,000 volumes including Asmita Publication textbooks, rare encyclopedias, classic literature, and international academic journals. Completely managed by a digital cataloging system.

Digital E-Library

Exclusive access to thousands of e-books, audiobooks, and academic papers. Students can check out Amazon Kindles for offline reading and utilize high-speed research terminals.

Study Environment

We feature designated silent reading zones, collaborative study pods with smartboards for group projects, and comfortable lounge seating for casual reading.
