Mathematics

Our Mathematics department is committed to dispelling math anxiety and fostering a deep love for logical problem-solving. We emphasize conceptual clarity over rote memorization.

Core Algebra

Linear equations, polynomials, quadratic formulas, and functions. We teach students to recognize the patterns underlying algebraic structures.

Geometry & Trig.

Spatial reasoning, trigonometric identities, proofs, and 3D coordinate geometry. Brought to life using interactive 3D modeling software.

Calculus & Stats

Limits, derivatives, integration, and probability distributions. Crucial foundations for students pursuing engineering, data science, or economics.
