Social Studies

History & Heritage

We explore ancient civilizations, global conflicts, and the rich local heritage of Nepal. Students learn to critically analyze historical sources and understand how the past inevitably shapes our current geopolitical landscape.

Geography & Environment

From plate tectonics and climate zones to human demographics and urban planning. We focus heavily on environmental conservation awareness and the socio-economic impacts of climate change.

Civics & Society

Understanding constitutional rights, government structures, and civic responsibilities. We bring theoretical civics to life through student council elections, mock parliaments, and active community service projects.
